Elementia Materiales is an international company engaged in the manufacture and marketing of construction materials. We have 27 plants across 9 countries - from the United States to Bolivia.

Our purpose as a company is to have "Happy Customers", which encompasses the entire value chain from investors, collaborators, distributors, and end customers. The best interests of our internal and external customers are at the heart of every decision we make.

We are a company guided by principles, behaviors, and passions, since Elementia Materiales is driven by its collaborators. Therefore, its greatest asset is its people. For this reason, we encourage our collaborators to "Think like owners", propose ideas and make things happen.
