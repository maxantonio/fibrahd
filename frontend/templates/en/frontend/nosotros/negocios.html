Building Systems

Mexalit

Mexalit specializes in lightweight solutions and water storage systems. It has 3 brands: i) Mexalit, specialized in the manufacture of fiber cement roofing tiles; ii) Eureka, focused on the manufacture of water tanks and cisterns with the most advanced rotational molding technology; and iii) Cempanel, specialized in the manufacture of fiber cement panels.

Learn more

Plycem

With 58 years in the construction industry, Plycem is a leading brand in the region, engaged in the manufacturing of fiber cement products to meet the building needs of the residential and commercial segments. The company is a pioneer in Central America in promoting an innovative construction system featuring Steel Framing and Plycem fiber cement, recognized for its strong commitment to sustainable building.

Learn more

Eternit Colombiana

For 80 years, Eternit Colombia has helped build the dreams of many generations of families in the country and has stood out as an ally in the delivery of comprehensive solutions with a high social and environmental impact, backed by the knowledge and experience gained through its ongoing efforts in the construction sector. Its business vision is rooted on the delivery of top-quality building solutions and integral systems for construction works and projects, designed through processes and strategies centered on innovation, sustainability, and inspiration for its clients and communities. Eternit, Construction in Evolution. Learn more

Eternit Ecuatoriana

Eternit Ecuador is a multinational company with 68 years in the market that delivers comprehensive solutions drawing on its knowledge and experience operating in the country. Its business vision is grounded on offering integral construction systems and implementing environmentally friendly sustainability strategies, hand in hand with continuous innovation processes and high technical standards.

Learn more

Frigocel

Frigocel has more than 50 years of experience offering smart and sustainable solutions for the plastics industry, mainly in the food, refrigeration, construction, automotive, manufacturing, household appliances, agricultural, advertising, and many other sectors. Sustainability is Frigocel’s main purpose, for this reason, through innovative and cutting-edge recycling processes, it reincorporates plastics to the production cycle and give them a second life, in addition to not using drinking water for its processes and generating zero CO2 emissions.

Learn more

Allura

Established in 2014 in the United States, Allura offers a broad range of building materials including lap sidings, panels, shingles, soffits, trims, and backerboards with the distinctive features and unmatched performance demanded by customers. In contrast to conventional building materials.
Allura products feature realistic wood grains and textures that come in a wide variety of factory-applied colors through its thicker, more uniform, and flawless pre-finish system that has twice the durability of field paint. The design possibilities are limitless!

Learn more

Fibraforte

Fibraforte is a leading Peruvian company engaged in the manufacturing and distribution of roof tiles, water tanks, and flat sheets. Since its inception in 1993, its main purpose has been to provide the construction market with innovative solutions that bring significant advantages and contribute to the sustainable development of Peru, hand in hand with a robust distribution network nationwide. At Fibraforte we make good things last.

Learn more

Comecop

Comecop is a Mexican company born to provide integral solutions for hydraulic infrastructure works, specialized in the manufacture of prestressed and reinforced concrete pipes to meet the most demanding needs since 1974. It is a company committed to the highest quality of its products, ensuring the hydraulic, structural, and chemical performance of the piping systems. Comecop guarantees the durability of its products in the most demanding project conditions. Its pipes are known for their excellent performance under combined loads and resistance to several chemicals, being the best option in the industry.

Duralit

Duralit began operations in 1977 in the city of Cochabamba with the vision of offering construction materials to improve Bolivia's quality of life. As pioneers in the fiber cement industry in Bolivia, we are an institutional benchmark in the region and the country due to our remarkable growth as a company, ongoing technological innovation, and compliance with applicable standards and laws.
We proved our ability to adapt to the ever-changing environment and needs of the Bolivian market, evolving over the past years into a company that provides comprehensive construction solutions, while broadening our product portfolio, providing technical advisory services, and spearheading the development of new construction systems that are changing the sector at a global scale. We have one of the most modern industrial facilities in the industry and a distribution network with more than 200 sale points throughout the country.
