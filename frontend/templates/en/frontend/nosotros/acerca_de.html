We have more than 20 years of experience in the construction industry

Elementia Materiales is a Mexican company with a worldwide presence engaged in lightweight construction and the development of innovative construction solutions used in all stages of construction.


Elementia Materiales was established in 2021, as a result of the spin-off of the Building Systems and Metals divisions from the Cement business (formerly Elementia S.A.B. de C.V.).

Business Units

Building Systems

This Business Unit is engaged in the manufacture and distribution of lightweight construction products and business solutions that meet the needs of the entry-level, middle-income, residential, and retail housing segments. Our experience is our backbone, as we have been integrating different business through mergers and acquisitions over the past twenty years.

Metals

Serves the needs of the industrial sector, mainly supporting original equipment manufacturers that work with metal alloys and components for the automotive, aeronautics, textile, electrical, and electronic industries, among others. Likewise, it meets the needs of the construction sector by manufacturing solutions and applications for water and gas equipment and piping.

Our Journey

Incorporation of Eternit Colombia

Established in 1942 and with extensive experience in the construction sector. It has 3 production plants in Colombia.

Incorporation of Duralit

Duralit began operations in 1977 in Bolivia and has one of the most modern production facilities in the industry and a distribution network with more than 200 points of sale.

Incorporation of Mexalit, Eureka

Mexalit began operations in Sta. Clara, State of Mexico in 1952, while Eureka was established in 1932.

Incorporation of Plycem

It was founded in 1964 in Costa Rica and has production facilities in Costa Rica, El Salvador, and Honduras.

Incorporation of Frigocel

Founded in 1984 in Mexico. It specializes in polystyrene products and solutions.

Incorporation of Fibraforte

Specialized in polypropylene products. Started operations in 1993 in Peru.

Incorporation of Nacobre

One of the most important copper manufacturers in Latin America. Established in 1950 in Mexico.

Incorporation of Allura

With more than 75 years of experience and 3 plants serving the U.S. market.
